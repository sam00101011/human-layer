# Verticals And Business

## High-leverage verticals

### 1. Developer internet

Best surfaces:
- GitHub
- docs sites
- Hacker News
- Product Hunt
- YouTube engineering channels
- arXiv

Why this is strong:
- users already care about signal quality
- people want summaries, trust, and discovery
- reputation can be built around real expertise

### 2. Crypto internet

Best surfaces:
- wallets
- dapps
- governance forums
- token pages
- research dashboards
- scam-prone pages

Why this is strong:
- bot and sybil problems are severe
- trust and scam detection matter
- one-human systems are especially valuable

### 3. Creator internet

Best surfaces:
- YouTube
- Substack
- blogs
- podcasts
- long-form essays

Why this is strong:
- users want "what is worth my time?"
- summaries and verdicts are valuable
- human curation beats algorithmic noise

### 4. Shopping and trust internet

Best surfaces:
- product pages
- marketplaces
- software tools
- review-heavy sites

Why this is strong:
- fake reviews and spam are everywhere
- verified-human commentary can be a major wedge
- verdicts and receipts can influence buying decisions

## Enterprise and B2B expansions

### Publisher widget

Add Human Layer directly to a publisher or community site.

Possible features:
- verified-human comments
- verdict chips
- summaries
- one-human voting
- moderation queues

### Community anti-sybil tools

Possible uses:
- one-human signups
- one-human giveaways
- one-human votes
- one-human waitlists
- one-human gated access

### Moderation API

Offer:
- verified-human juries
- moderation appeals
- reputation-based trust weighting
- unique-human rate limits

### Discovery API

Offer:
- find real humans interested in a topic
- find high-signal humans in a category
- recommended experts by domain

### Trust layer for marketplaces

Offer:
- verified-human reviewers
- raters
- judges
- arbitrators
- appeal panels

## Strong future concepts

### Human-only internet mode

A mode where:
- ads disappear
- native comments collapse
- Human Layer commentary becomes the default social layer

### Verified-human overlay on any site

Treat Human Layer as a universal trust and discourse layer for the open web.

### Search by real-human agreement

Rank pages, products, and people by verified-human usefulness rather than bot amplification or SEO alone.

### Agent checkpoints

Require a verified human checkpoint before certain actions:
- high-risk agent actions
- payouts
- publishing
- governance actions

### One-human rights systems

Use Human Layer as infrastructure for:
- grants
- invites
- rebates
- claims
- jury seats
- waitlists

## Why this matters

As Human Layer expands, the business gets stronger because the same primitives can power:
- consumer products
- community tools
- publisher integrations
- trust infrastructure
- marketplaces
- agent systems

That makes the product bigger than a browser add-on.
